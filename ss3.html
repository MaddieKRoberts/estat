High School Social Studies salary at Baltimore County Public Schools

The average high school social studies salary at Baltimore County Public Schools in 2025 was $85,213 . High School Social Studies salaries at Baltimore County Public Schools typically range from $68,730 to $100,066 (25th to 75th percentile). The highest reported high school social studies salary was $120,717. There are 260 employees at Baltimore County Public Schools with the job title high school social studies.
